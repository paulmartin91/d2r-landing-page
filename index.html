What is D2R?

Drags 2 Riches is a 2-4 player deck-building game where you get to live out your drag queen truth. Players will build their outfit collection over the rounds, each turn rifling through their wardrobes to lay down the realest looks and win more fame than those other busted queens. How you win is up to you. Will you play a new look every turn, hoping to claim the circuit through persistence and hard work? Or will you take your time and build your look over several rounds, saving the big reveal for an extravaganza worth your time? Whatever you decide, be careful hunty, because only the queen with fiercest look will take home the prize.
