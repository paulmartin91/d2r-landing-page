Tell me more queen!

Drags 2 Riches is the only game that lets you live your drag truth.

First Choose your Queen

Each queen has her own special traits. Will you be a fierce glamazon, a boujee bitch, or a shady ho?

Build your wardrobe…

Splash your hard earned cash and add some new outfits, shoes and accessories to your collection.

…and work on your showstopping look

Use your strongest items to form a look fit for the gods! Work on it in secret until it’s perfect.

Turn up at the events and outshine the rest!

Will you play a new look every turn, hoping to claim the circuit through persistence and hard work?

Or will you take your time and build your look over several rounds, saving the big reveal for an extravaganza worth your time?

Whatever you decide, be careful hunty, because some of these shady divas will do whatever it takes to win!
